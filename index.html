Reconnecting Campus,
One Item at a Time.

We've all been there—that sinking feeling when you realize your keys, wallet, or favorite notebook is missing. Campus Lost & Found was born from a simple necessity: the need for a centralized, reliable way to recover what matters most.

Our mission is to replace scattered group chat messages with a streamlined digital hub. By leveraging real-time reporting and community trust, we turn the panic of loss into the relief of recovery.
